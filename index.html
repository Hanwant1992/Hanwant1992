About Me

I am a Research Associate at the Department of Botany, Mohanlal Sukhadia University, Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. My research spans plant stress physiology, molecular biology, phytoremediation, nanotechnology, duckweed, and photosynthesis. I am dedicated to advancing plant science through innovative research and collaboration.
